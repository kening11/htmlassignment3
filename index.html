Safari Zoo Run'19

Date: 24th February 2019

Time: 7AM-11AM

Venue: Singapore Zoo, River Safari, Night Safari

Run For Wildlife

The Safari Zoo Run was first organised to commemorate one of Singapore's most loved animal - Ah Meng, the orangutan, who passed on in 2008 due to old age.
The event is then organised yearly to empower runners to support wildlife conservation. Join the race to experience this unique opportunity to run through Singapore Zoo, River Safari and Night Safari in the day with family and friends!
As the run embarks on its 11th year, runners can expect more this year with the introduction of new race categories and sustainable initiatives!

New Race Routes & Categories

  • Safari Zoo Challenge - Competitive run is now 2km longer at 12km! It's time to put your endurance to the test.
  • This year, Safari Zoo Duo Dash and Safari Zoo Junior Dash are introduced for adults to pair up with their kids to form a team.
  • Safari Zoo Fun Walk for those who want to enjoy a leisurely pace with the family, it is good for little ones in strollers too!
  • River Safari will be open as a route for Safari Zoo Walk! Here is your chance to visit the river dwellers and say hi to Canola in Amazon Flooded Forest!
